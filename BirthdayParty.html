The number 1 song on December 19, 2003 was....

"Hey Ya!"

by Outkast

This song had just begun its 9 week stay at the number one spot on the Billboard Hot 100 when this day hit! The high-energy song became a hit pop song and would be highlighted as Outkast's "catchiest song" according to Entertainment Weekly. This song became the first to reach 1 million downloads on iTunes! One of the most popular lyrics, "shake it like a Polaroid picture", help to revitalize the company Polaroid, who had declared bankruptcy in 2001.
